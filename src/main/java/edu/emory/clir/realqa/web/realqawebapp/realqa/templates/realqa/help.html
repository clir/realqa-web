The home icon button will bring you to your Dashboard, which lists recommended questions which can be sorted by clicking on the Sort By button.

To ask a question, enter your question into the ask bar, and click post to submit it. You can also tag your question by entering a space and then a hashtag before each tag.

You can view questions under each tag by clicking on a tag listed underneath the question.

To view a question and it's answers, click on the question text. Here you can enter an answer for the question in text box, and submit it by clicking the Post button.

Click on the up arrow to upvote a post, and click on the down arrow to downvote a post. Click on the same arrow again to undo the action. You can't vote on your own posts.

To access your messages, click on the mail icon.

To access your profile, which includes your asked questions, answered questions, and subscribed questions, click on the gear icon.

To log out, click on the Log out button.
